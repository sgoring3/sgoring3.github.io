Sara Anne Goring

PhD Graduate Student in Applied Cognitive Psychology

Claremont Graduate University

Sara Anne Goring is a PhD graduate student at Claremont Graduate University. Her research interests include language, rationality, intelligence, and aging. Additionally her research involves statistical modeling techniques like latent variable modeling and psychometric network analysis.
